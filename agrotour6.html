AGROTOURISM SCHEME:

The Agrotourism Scheme is a government program designed to promote rural tourism by integrating agriculture and tourism activities. It encourages farmers to diversify their income streams by offering tourists experiences such as farm stays, agricultural tours, and hands-on farming activities. The scheme aims to boost rural economies, create employment opportunities, and preserve cultural heritage. Through this initiative, tourists gain insights into traditional farming practices while farmers generate additional revenue through tourism-related activities.
