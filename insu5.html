Experimental Crop Insurance Scheme:

The Experimental Crop Insurance Scheme is a pilot initiative designed to test innovative approaches to crop insurance. It incorporates advanced risk assessment models and technology to accurately evaluate and mitigate agricultural risks. Through this scheme, participating farmers have access to customized insurance products tailored to their specific needs and circumstances. The program aims to enhance the effectiveness and efficiency of crop insurance, ultimately improving resilience in the agricultural sector. Evaluation of the pilot's outcomes will inform potential expansion or integration into broader insurance programs.
