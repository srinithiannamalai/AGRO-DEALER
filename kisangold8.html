KISAN GOLD CARD SCHEME:

The Kisan Credit Card (KCC) scheme is a government initiative in India aimed at providing farmers with easy access to credit for their agricultural and allied activities. It offers farmers a credit card facility with a pre-approved loan limit based on their land holdings and cropping patterns. The KCC scheme enables farmers to meet their short-term credit requirements for purchasing seeds, fertilizers, pesticides, and other inputs, as well as for meeting operational expenses. It simplifies the loan application process and reduces the dependency on informal sources of credit, thereby promoting financial inclusion and rural development. The scheme empowers farmers by providing timely and affordable credit, enhancing agricultural productivity, and improving their standard of living.
