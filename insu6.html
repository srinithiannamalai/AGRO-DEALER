Comprehensive crop insurance:

Comprehensive crop insurance provides coverage against a wide range of risks including natural disasters, adverse weather conditions, pests, and diseases. It offers financial protection to farmers by compensating for crop losses, ensuring their economic stability. Premium rates are often subsidized by the government to make it accessible to farmers. The insurance scheme plays a crucial role in safeguarding agricultural livelihoods and promoting risk management in the farming sector. By mitigating losses, it fosters confidence among farmers, encouraging investment in agriculture and enhancing food security.
