Cultivation Process of Guava Tree

    Step 1: Selection of Variety

    Choose a guava variety suitable for your climate and preferences.

    Step 2: Planting Location

    Select a sunny spot with well-drained soil.

    Step 3: Soil Preparation

    Prepare the soil by loosening it up and adding organic matter like compost.

    Step 4: Planting

    Plant the guava tree during spring or early summer months.

    Step 5: Watering

    Water deeply but infrequently, ensuring the soil remains moist.

    Step 6: Fertilizing

    Apply a balanced fertilizer regularly during the growing season.

    Step 7: Pruning

    Prune the tree to maintain shape and remove dead or diseased branches.

    Step 8: Pest and Disease Management

    Monitor for pests and diseases and take appropriate control measures.

    Step 9: Harvesting

    Harvest ripe guavas by gently twisting them from the tree.
