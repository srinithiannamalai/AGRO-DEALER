MICROIRRIGATION FUND SCHEME:

The Microirrigation Fund Scheme is a government initiative aimed at promoting water-efficient irrigation practices among farmers. It provides financial assistance for the adoption of microirrigation systems such as drip and sprinkler irrigation. The scheme helps farmers reduce water wastage, improve crop yields, and enhance overall agricultural productivity. Eligible beneficiaries can avail subsidies for the installation of microirrigation infrastructure on their farms. By encouraging sustainable water management, the scheme contributes to water conservation and agricultural sustainability.
