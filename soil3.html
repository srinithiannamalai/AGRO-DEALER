SOIL HEALTH SCHEME:

The Soil Health Card scheme is a government initiative in India aimed at promoting sustainable farming practices. It provides farmers with personalized soil health cards containing information about the nutrient status of their soil. This data helps farmers make informed decisions about fertilizers and agricultural inputs, optimizing yield and reducing input costs. By promoting soil health, the scheme aims to enhance agricultural productivity and sustainability while preserving natural resources. It also encourages farmers to adopt soil conservation practices for long-term environmental benefits.
