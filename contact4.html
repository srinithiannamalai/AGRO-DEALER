Arjun Munda

Arjun Munda (born 3 May 1968) is an Indian politician. He is the current Minister of Tribal Affairs and Minister of Agriculture and Farmers' Welfare in the Second Modi ministry.

Kailash Choudhary

Kailash Choudhary (born 20 September 1973) is an Indian politician who is current Minister of State for Agriculture and Farmers' Welfare in Second Modi ministry.
