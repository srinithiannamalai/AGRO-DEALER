first individual approach crop insurance

The first individual approach crop insurance marks a shift from collective risk-sharing to personalized coverage tailored to individual farmers. It utilizes advanced data analytics and technology to assess risks specific to each farmer's crop production, reducing moral hazard and adverse selection. This approach ensures fairer premiums and more accurate payouts based on individual circumstances, enhancing farmers' trust in the insurance system. By incentivizing prudent farming practices and mitigating losses effectively, it contributes to sustainable agricultural development and resilience in the face of uncertainties.
